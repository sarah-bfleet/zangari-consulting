Speaker, Entrepreneur, Founder, Creativity Coach, Innovation Catalyst

Jessica will show you how to tap into your own creativity and how to translate that creativity to big ideas. She speaks about how to stand out, breakthrough, shine and thrive at work - allowing one to love their life and love their work.

Jessica delivers in-person and virtual speeches that empower everyone with the tools they need to start innovating today.

About Jessica

Straight out of undergrad, Jessica took a part time tutoring job and transformed that into an academic prep business that employed a team of 50 people. After successfully selling her start up, she went to Columbia Business School and earned her MBA. She put that degree to use as a management consultant and innovation strategist where she worked with Fortune 500 executives to develop new products and services as well as helped organizations learn how to foster creativity and nurture more innovative corporate cultures. Jessica has delivered speeches, workshops and coaching for clients including Coca Cola, Pfizer, American Express, Hasbro, Johnson and Johnson, LVMH, and Hearst among many others. She has spoken to audiences of hundreds of people throughout the world.

She now brings her expertise to audiences using a unique approach helping people capture unlikely connections, leading to fresh thinking and disruptive ideas. Everyone will learn practical skills on how to actually come up with new ideas, how to embody the behaviors that foster creativity, and the practical frameworks to implement those ideas that can be applied immediately.
